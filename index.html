Fix 1 · StructuralCut the gap from 4 weeks to 2 weeks

The single biggest lever. In 2025 the paid physical summit meant limited cold audience, requiring a longer highlights window. In 2026 the free summit delivers far more cold audience live — so the window can be halved. Two weeks eliminates the two worst buyer's remorse weeks. Everything below fills those two weeks with value so buyers arrive at Week 1 already activated.

Fix 2 · Highest impactThe Bonus Workshop — first 500 buyers, weekend after the summit

A 4-hour live workshop the weekend after the summit closes. Not a kick-off call. A real session — the kind people would pay separately to attend. First 500 buyers get access.

The hook
"Buy within [X hours] and you're in the bonus workshop next Saturday. Even if you decide to refund within 14 days, the workshop recording is yours to keep." Removes the purchase barrier — they experience value before fully committing.
Why it works
A buyer who has sat in a live 4-hour session, felt something, connected with other students — does not refund. The "keep even if you refund" framing paradoxically makes them not want to refund.
The urgency
500 spots. Real limit. Every cart email counts down: "412 of 500 spots remaining." Drives early purchase through community scarcity, not a countdown timer.
Fix 3 · Immediate valueThe Attunement — 7-day pre-programme meditation series on the app

Unlocks immediately on purchase. Meditations already exist on the Mindvalley app. Three meditations in rotation before the programme starts. Someone who has done 7 days of daily meditation before Week 1 has invested time, not just money. Time investment is harder to walk away from.

Days 1–2
Receiving meditation — primes the nervous system to receive rather than chase. Ties into Sonia's surrender arc and what Week 1 opens with.
Days 3–4
Wealth frequency activation — the energetic state Regan works with. Quiz buyers have already started this in the quiz funnel — the Attunement deepens it.
Days 5–7
Identity meditation — "I am the version of myself who has already manifested this." Sets the frame Vishen opens Mastery Week 1 with. They arrive already in the right state.
Day 3 email
"You've done 3 days of Attunement. Notice anything different? The programme goes 10x deeper starting [date]." A milestone acknowledgement that makes them feel progress is already happening.
Fix 4Community activation — Finer Minds within hours of purchase

Every buyer is added to the Mastery cohort group on Finer Minds within hours of purchase. First message they receive: "Welcome to the Class of 2026. Introduce yourself — one sentence: I'm here because I want ___."

Once someone has introduced themselves to a community, they feel social obligation to stay. The cohort name — Class of 2026 — creates an identity they belong to before they've done a single session. People don't leave groups they identify with. Finer Minds is also where the 7-day Attunement check-ins happen, making it immediately active rather than just a passive group.

Fix 5Personal outreach within 24 hours of purchase

A real human from the Mastery team sends a short personal message to every new buyer within 24 hours: "I saw you just joined. What's the one thing you most want to experience in the programme?"

People do not refund after a real human has paid attention to them. This is the highest-ROI refund prevention action that exists. At 1,500 students it requires a team — but even a semi-personalised message from a named team member is dramatically better than any automated sequence.
